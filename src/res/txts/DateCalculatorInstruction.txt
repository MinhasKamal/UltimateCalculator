Date Calculator Instruction:
This mode can calculte difference between any two dates from 01/01/0001 to 31/12/1000000.


# Enter your first date in the 'From' text fields (dd, mm, yy).

# The program automatically writes today's date in the 'To' text fields. You may over-write it if necessary.

# Press button 'Calculate' to get the difference.


Extreme Case: Sometimes you may see the program processing invalid inputs, so then it will be up to you to give right inputs.
